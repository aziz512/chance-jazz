Chance Jazz is a random chord and scale generator aimed to help you become better at improvisation by testing your musical knowledge, creativity and reaction times.

This website was inspired by Warren Burt's "Without Glue" program and series of works. It shows each chord or scale for a number of seconds before changing.

Originally built by u/MONGEN_beats. Revived and modified by @aziz512

Chance jazz tests your musical knowledge with a random set of scales and chords.
Improvise over each idea as it countdowns to the next.
There are no wrong answers, just play what you see.

We want to see your chops! Comment thread for your improvisations coming soon!
